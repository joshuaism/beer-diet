About Beer Diet

Inspired by the stories of certain monestaries forgoing solid food and partaking of a beer only diet for the lenten season, Beer Diet exists to inform its users of the nutritional value of our favorite naturally carbonated beverages and consider the feasability of adopting such a fast for themselves.

For that purpose Beer Diet has assembled a list of beers available in the US market along with their calorie counts, abvs, and any other available information regarding the nutritional content of those beers. So let Beer Diet be your guide how well you would fare should you subsist on the single beverage of your choice!
